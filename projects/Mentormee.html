Mentormee provides a free mobile platform for high school students to connect with university students to help them foster an engaging and personal mentorship opportunity. It is a great opportunity for university students to gain meaningful leadership experiences. At the same time, high school students get direct benefit from their new connections. Mentormee leverages today's mobile technology and algorithms to build the most exclusive online mentorship program in the world.

The mobile application was coded in Objective-C. User information was stored in a SQL server and a PHP web service was used to communicate between the server and the application.

Mentormee was created out of the University of Toronto's startup incubator The Hatchery. Mentormee was a finalist and pitched to angels and VCs at demo day.

Mentormee is an ongoing project that strives to help students understand what they want to get out of their post secondary experience. If you would like to help with our vision feel free to contact me or check out our website!
