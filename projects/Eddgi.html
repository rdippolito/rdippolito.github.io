Eddgi is an arcade strategy game that requires players to accumulate points by collecting coins and avoiding the evil edged shapes! As the player advances the number and frequency of enemies increase. Unlock your favorite characters and climb the highscores.

Eddgi was built in Swift using SpriteKit. The highscores are managed with Parse. The artwork was done using Sketch. The application, characters and scenes were kept simple and intuitive to optimize the players experience.
